About Shopmaiah

Hi! I’m Shemaiah Dela Cruz, the founder of Shopmaiah an online shop created with love for fashion and smart shopping. I’m also an IT student, and through this small business, I combine my passion for style with my goal of becoming more independent and entrepreneurial.

Shopmaiah offers a carefully selected mix of thrift clothes, preloved items, and brand-new pieces. Every item is chosen to be stylish, affordable, and worth your money. Whether you’re looking for trendy outfits, budget-friendly finds, or unique pieces, you’ll find something special here.
